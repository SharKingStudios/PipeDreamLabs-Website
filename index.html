About Us

At Pipe Dream Labs, we specialize in custom PCB design and innovative electrical engineering. Founded and run by Logan Peterson, our one-man powerhouse wearing many hats: engineering, administration, and business management. We believe in bringing even the wildest pipe dreams to life—because sometimes, that’s exactly what engineering is all about.

Services

Custom PCB Design

Schematics, layout, manufacturing prep—our boards are crafted to meet your specs. Solder those pipe dreams in place!

Electrical System Engineering

Full-stack hardware solutions, from conceptual circuits to integrated systems. Let us connect the wires so you don’t have to.

Prototyping & Consulting

Let’s build a working proof-of-concept and iterate until your vision is real. No project is too big—nor too much of a "pipe dream".

How We Work

1. Discuss Your Goals

Tell us about your dream project—whether it’s a quick fix or the next big invention. We’ll hash out the specs.

2. Design & Prototype

We design the circuit or PCB layout, provide prototypes, and gather your feedback to refine the project.

3. Build & Deliver

Once finalized, we handle manufacturing, testing, and deliver a ready-to-go product or a blueprint you can run with.
